Introduction to Soutei

Soutei is Policy Decision Point (PDP). It offers advice about what actions are permitted, according to a policy. Enforcement of policy is left to a Policy Enforcement Point (PEP).

Design goals:

  • Universal policy language and authorization service
  • Delegated and decentralized policies
  • High-assurance (Common Criteria Evaluation Assurance Level 6)

This demonstration was first given in June 2005.
